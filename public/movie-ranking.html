My Top Three Movies of All Time

Come and See

Beautifully shot and appropriately terrifying, a war film that plays out more like a horror film.

Ikiru

My favorite Kurosawa, very humanistic and a meditation on the meaning of life, from a man at the end of his.

Stalker

Meditative and beautifully shot, an philosophical examination of life between three characters, named Stalker, Writer, and Professor
